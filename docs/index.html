Our belief

The best technology disappears.

Carmel Valley taught us something worth carrying everywhere: heritage, community, and a high standard of experience are worth protecting. Too often technology works against those things — adding friction, flattening character, leaving people behind. We believe it can do the opposite. Applied with care, it strengthens what people value rather than overrunning it, and brings everyone along instead of leaving anyone behind.

Community

Technology that brings people together and reinforces the ties that make a business, and a place, feel like home.

Heritage

Solutions that respect the character and history of what you've built, rather than paving over what makes it singular.

Experience

Friction removed at every turn, so every customer, member, and visit feels effortless and considered.

About

Silicon Valley experience, brought home to the Peninsula.

Carmel Valley Technologies was founded by Chris Coyoli, who spent his career in Silicon Valley technology — in operations and systems roles at companies whose products are used by millions, with a deep stretch in enterprise software and fintech. The throughline was always the same: making complex technology dependable, useful, and genuinely worth it.

He started this firm to bring that capability home — to the community he's part of. The businesses of the Monterey Peninsula set the standard for hospitality and experience; they deserve technology that meets it, applied at a human scale and with real respect for the place.
